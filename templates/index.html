About Me

My journey as a Full Stack Software Engineer didn’t start in a traditional computer science lab, it started at the intersection of visual design and digital marketing. I began my career as a digital media specialist, mastering tools like WordPress, SEO, and the Adobe Creative Suite to help businesses grow their online presence.

However, my passion for creative problem-solving and learning new technologies naturally pushed me deeper into the code. I wanted to build the engines behind the interfaces. Today, I bridge the gap between creative design and complex backend architecture.

Because of my background, I approach software engineering a bit differently. I leverage my marketing experience to understand exactly what clients and end-users need, and I use my technical stack to build it. I pride myself on being an adaptable project manager who can juggle multiple moving parts and translate complex technical concepts into plain, understandable terms. Whether I'm optimizing a front-end UI or architecting an AI-integrated backend, my goal is always the same: to deliver innovative, accessible, and high-performing digital experiences.
